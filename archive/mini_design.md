## Typography
- Ops Console & Staff App: Space Grotesk (geometric, industrial)
- Fan App: Inter (humanist, warm)
- Numeric Display: IBM Plex Mono

## Layout Patterns
- Ops Console: Three-column dashboard, dense metrics.
- Fan App: Mobile-first, card-based.
- Staff App: Neo-brutalist, massive touch targets.
